# Release v14.1.8

## Fix — Weapon RR wrongly applied to attack rolls

An item's Risk Reduction entries were applied to that item's own rolls unconditionally, regardless of what skill each RR entry actually targets. A weapon like the Shiawase Arms Puzzler (RR 2 on Stealth, to conceal it) wrongly granted its RR to the ranged attack roll made with it.

- An item's RR entry now applies to the item's own roll only if it has no target (generic RR), or if it targets the skill, specialization, or attribute actually used for that roll.
- Entries targeting another skill (e.g. Stealth) keep applying to that skill's rolls, as before.
- Also removes a potential double count when a weapon's RR entry targets its own attack skill (it was counted both as item RR and as skill RR).
- Fixed in the character sheet display (weapon RR badge), the roll dialog opened from the sheet, and the weapon selector inside the roll dialog. Applies to weapons, spells, complex forms, cyberdecks, and vehicle weapons.

Thanks to L34ndr3 and Ackinty for the report and diagnosis.
