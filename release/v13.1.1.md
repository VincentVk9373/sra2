# Release v13.1.1

**Shadowrun Anarchy 2 — FoundryVTT System**

Hotfix release addressing two issues discovered after v13.1.0.

---

## Bug Fixes

### Spells and Complex Forms now support multi-target attacks
Spells, weapon-spells and complex forms were not recognized as attacks in the chat message, preventing defend and counter-attack buttons from appearing when targeting multiple tokens. The `isAttack` detection now includes all attack-capable item types.

### Fixed specialization naming for Emerged specializations
Corrected a spacing inconsistency in Emerged specialization names (`Spé:` vs `Spé :`) that could prevent the system from matching them correctly.
