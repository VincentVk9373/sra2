# Release v14.1.7

## Roll Dialog — Dice colored by risk level

- Each die in the dice pool is now colored according to the risk level it would produce if selected as a risk die: green (low to no risk), blue (normal), yellow (high), red (extreme).
- The risk thresholds are visible at a glance directly on the dice row — no need to click through each selection to find them.
- Selected risk dice keep the purple highlight effect; their icon is tinted with the die's own risk color (blue tint added for normal risk).
- Risk level thresholds unchanged: low (<2% critical glitch & <0.2% disaster), normal (<7.5% & <1%), high (<20% & <5%), extreme above.

## Character Sheet V2

- Bio / Notes editor area is taller (min height 250px instead of 80px), applied to both the rendered content and the active ProseMirror editor.

## FR Translation Fixes

- "Magic" → "Magique" (monitor gauge type and weapon/matrix damage types).
- Server sheet: "ICE" → "GLACE" for consistency with the rest of the FR translation ("Déployer une GLACE", "GLACES hébergées", "GLACE ajoutée/retirée", "Aucune GLACE disponible à déployer", "Glissez une GLACE ici pour l'ajouter au serveur").
